**Topic:** What we wish every founder knew before spending money on marketing

![image](./image.jpg)

## Caption

Ever wondered if your marketing budget is really working for you? Many founders feel like they're guessing when it comes to spending on promotion, and that's okay — but it doesn't have to stay that way.

Before you invest a single dollar, take a moment to get clear on three things: Who are you trying to reach, what specific problem are you solving for them, and what does 'success' actually look like for this marketing effort? Knowing your audience and goals is your secret weapon. 🎯

Think of your marketing spend as an experiment. Start small, test what resonates, learn from the data, and then scale what works. Using AI-powered insights can help you understand your audience better and optimize your campaigns, making every dollar work harder. It's about smart, intentional growth.
